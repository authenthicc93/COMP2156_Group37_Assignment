COMP 2156 course at George Brown College focuses on DevOps practices, 
tools, and methods, aiming to bridge the gap between development 
and operations teams.

It topics the 	
	
	- Containerization with Docker, Automating deployments with Jenkins
	Cloud, and CI/CD.
	- Implementing monitoring systems and logging practices ensures real time
	 tracking of application perfromance and identification of issues, while scaling and load balancing techniques.
