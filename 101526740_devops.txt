COMP 3104, titled "DevOps," is a course at George Brown College that focuses on the principles and practices of DevOps, 
aiming to equip students with the skills necessary to integrate development and operations for efficient software 
delivery. Prerequisites for enrolling in COMP 3104 include successful completion of COMP 3044 (Linux Essentials). 
The course is delivered in a hybrid format, combining online and in-person instruction.
