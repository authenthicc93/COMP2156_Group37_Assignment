## George Brown College 
George Brown College (GBC) is a public college in Toronto, Canada, offering a wide range of diploma, certificate, and degree programs across business, technology, health sciences, hospitality, and the arts. 
Known for its strong industry connections, hands-on learning, and emphasis on applied research, GBC provides students with real-world experience through co-op placements and field education. 
The college has three main campuses—St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront—each specializing in different disciplines. With a diverse student body and a focus on career readiness, 
George Brown College is a leading institution for practical, skills-based education in Canada.
